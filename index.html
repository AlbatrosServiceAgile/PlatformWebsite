Some examples
for many solutions

Quality Assurance Inspection Software

A custom software solution was developed to address a manufacturing client's quality assurance challenges, which included time-consuming manual processes and human error. The implemented system optimized quality control through real-time tracking, automated alerts, and detailed inspection reports, resulting in reduced inspection times and improved product quality.

Equipment Inventory and Calibration Certificate

A custom software solution was developed for an industrial rental company to address challenges in managing heavy machinery inventory, customer relationships, and equipment calibration standards. The integrated system, comprising inventory management, CRM, and calibration certificate tools, significantly improved operational efficiency, customer satisfaction, and regulatory compliance, replacing outdated Excel-based processes.

Health and Safety Inspection Checklist

A comprehensive digital solution was developed to replace a construction company's inefficient paper-based health and safety inspection processes. The system consists of a web application for creating customized checklists and managing data, and a mobile app for conducting on-site inspections with photo documentation, enabling real-time reporting and efficient management of safety compliance across multiple construction sites.

Building Materials On-Site Delivery

An integrated digital solution was created for a construction materials supplier to address urgent material shortages and prevent project delays. The system combines a web app for managers with mobile apps for clients and delivery crews, featuring smart notifications to prioritize urgent orders and enable rapid delivery of specialized materials, significantly reducing project timelines and improving efficiency.
